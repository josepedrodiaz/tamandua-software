Our story

Built by hand.
Passed down with care.

Some families pass down recipes. Others pass down songs. In our family, we pass down code.

"He taught her to code. She reminded him why he started."

Tamandua Software was founded by Pedro and Oderay -- father and daughter, senior architect and rising developer, fifteen years of battle-tested experience sitting across the desk from the kind of restless curiosity that only comes from seeing the craft for the first time.

When you work with Tamandua, you're trusting your project to two people who will argue over the right way to structure your database at the dinner table. Who take your deadline personally because their name -- the same name -- is on the work.

Pedro brings the scar tissue of experience -- knowing what will break before it breaks. Oderay brings the energy of someone still amazed by what a well-written function can do. Between us, we have the patience to do it right and the hunger to do it now.
